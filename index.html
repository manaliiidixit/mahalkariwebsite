About us

Spices have a long and ancient history, especially in india, where they are part of life and heritage. In every home and every province across the country, different spices and blends are used to create diffren tastes in dishes. Several decades ago housewifes used to grind their spices manually at home and make their own blend for use of their cooking. To make their process easier for housewifes we are here with the concept of ready-to-use ground spices.
